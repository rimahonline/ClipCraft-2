# **App Name**: ClipCraft

## Core Features:

- AI Content Generator: Users provide a topic or keywords, and the AI tool generates creative text, script ideas, and content suggestions tailored for social media reels and posts.
- Template Library: Browse and select from a library of professionally designed templates optimized for various social media platforms (e.g., Instagram Reel, TikTok, Facebook Post).
- Media Upload & Integration: Easily upload personal photos and videos from device storage, with basic in-app tools for resizing and positioning within chosen templates.
- Text & Graphic Overlays: Add and customize text overlays with a selection of fonts and colors, along with simple graphic elements, to enhance content.
- Direct Export & Share: Export finalized reels and posts in high-quality video or image formats, or share directly to popular social media platforms.
- Creative Project Dashboard: A centralized interface to view, manage, and continue working on saved content drafts and completed projects.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual design draws inspiration from creativity and modern digital media. A light color scheme ensures content visibility and a clean user experience.
- Primary Color: A vibrant, medium blue (#478FEB) represents innovation and clarity, used for key interactive elements and branding accents.
- Background Color: A very pale, cool blue-grey (#ECEEF2) provides a clean, unobtrusive canvas, ensuring user-generated content stands out.
- Accent Color: A bright, energetic cyan (#52D4E6) creates strong visual contrast and highlights calls to action or important notifications.
- Headline and Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) is chosen for its modern, clear, and objective aesthetic, ensuring high readability for social media-focused content.
- Use clean, contemporary line icons with a clear visual metaphor, maintaining simplicity and ensuring quick comprehension for users navigating creation tools.
- Mobile-first, intuitive layout with a focus on large tap targets and a clear hierarchy of information to streamline the content creation workflow.
- Subtle, fluid transitions and micro-interactions provide satisfying visual feedback for user actions, such as saving content or applying edits, enhancing the overall user experience without distraction.
